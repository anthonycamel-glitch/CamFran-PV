Uber in Puerto Vallarta

Uber is reliable and widely available throughout Puerto Vallarta.

  • Transportation throughout the city
  • Airport pickups
  • Restaurant delivery with Uber Eats
  • Grocery delivery from stores such as Casa Ley
  • Pharmacy, convenience store, and household supply deliveries

Most food, grocery, and supply deliveries arrive within approximately 30 minutes, depending on traffic, store availability, and time of day.
